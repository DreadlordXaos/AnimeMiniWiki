Introduction

Anime is a style of animation originating in Japan, known for its artistic visuals, emotional storytelling, and diverse genres. Anime includes television series, films, and web productions.

Unlike Western animation, anime targets audiences of all ages and often explores mature themes.

Key Characteristics

  • Expressive character designs
  • Detailed backgrounds
  • Wide genre diversity
